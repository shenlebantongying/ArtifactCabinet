\section{Special functions}

\subsection{The factorial function}

Factorial can be represented in terms of a continuous function with $e$:
\begin{align*}
  \int_{0}^{\infty}x^{n}e^{-a x}\,dx&=\frac{n!}{a^{n+1}} \\
  \int_{0}^{\infty}x^{n}e^{-x}\,dx&=n!.
\end{align*}
